Chicago crime dataset analysis using gcp

Data

The dataset is provided by City of Chicago(https://data.cityofchicago.org/Public-Safety/Crimes- 2001-to-Present/ijzp-q8t2) and reflects reported incidents of crime from 2001 to present. Our data-set has around 4 million rows and is 1 GB in size. The data has 22 columns.Some of the columns relevant to this project are: ID, Case number, Date, Block, Primary Type, Latitude, Longitude.
